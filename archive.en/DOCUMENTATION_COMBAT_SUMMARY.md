## 🎯 Key Points Documented

### Positioning (VERY IMPORTANT)
```
⚠️ Positions 0-2: Melee (front row)
✅ Positions 3+: Ranged/Spells (back row)

Spellcasters MUST be in position 3+ to cast spells!
```

### Automatic Features of CombatSystem
1. 🔮 Character spellcasting (if positioned correctly)
2. 👹 Monster spellcasting (Mage, Lich, etc.)
3. ⚔️ Attacks with equipped weapons
4. 🎯 Special attacks (dragon breath, multi-attack)
5. 🩹 Automatic healing (spells and potions)
6. 📊 Tactical intelligence (action priorities)

### Equipment
- Weapons: "punches" (1-2) → "slashes" (5-10)
- Armor: AC 10 → AC 14-16
- Equipment drastically increases effectiveness

### Real Output Examples

#### With Equipment
